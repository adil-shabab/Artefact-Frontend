Artefact is a construction, architecture, and interior design firm based in India that transforms spaces into functional and aesthetically pleasing environments. We aim to design and build meaningful spaces that reflect our clients' visions while ensuring durability and practicality.
Our mission focuses on creating balanced, healthy, and beautiful living and working spaces for our clients, enhancing their quality of life and experience for years to come.

Our Approach process

Our team conducts a thorough survey to understand your needs and preferences. We provide a detailed quote outlining the project scope, costs, and timelines to ensure transparency and clarity.
We handle all aspects of construction, ensuring quality workmanship and adherence to timelines. Our skilled team uses the best materials and practices to bring your project to life.
Our architectural services include creating innovative and functional designs. We provide technical drawings, 3D images, and VR experiences to help you visualize your project before construction begins.
Our interior design team crafts beautiful and functional spaces tailored to your style. We offer comprehensive design solutions, including furniture selection, color schemes, and decor to create a harmonious environment.
Upon project completion, we conduct a final inspection and hand over the keys. We ensure everything meets your expectations and provide support for any post-completion needs.
